Vector databases enable approximate nearest neighbor (ANN) search over high-dimensional embeddings.

## Pinecone

Fully managed, serverless option with excellent developer experience but limited self-hosting.

## Weaviate

Open-source with built-in vectorization modules and GraphQL API.

## Qdrant

Rust-based, high-performance engine with rich filtering capabilities.

## Milvus

Distributed architecture supporting billions of vectors with GPU acceleration.

## ChromaDB

Lightweight, Python-native option ideal for prototyping and small-scale applications.
